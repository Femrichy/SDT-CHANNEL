Who We Are

Engineering muscle behind Nigeria's biggest projects

THE SDT CHANNEL INT'L LTD is a freight-forwarding, cargo-logistics, and industrial equipment company built to keep construction, oil & gas, mining, and manufacturing operations running. We source genuine Caterpillar® parts, maintain a diesel generator fleet for rent or sale, and provide the preventive maintenance that keeps heavy machinery out of the workshop and on the job.
