Built on parts that don't fail and promises that don't slip

THE SDT CHANNEL INT'L LTD started as a freight-forwarding and cargo-logistics operation and grew into a full industrial parts and power-solutions company because our clients kept asking for one thing: someone who could move heavy equipment logistics and heavy equipment parts with the same reliability.

Our Story

From cargo logistics to full industrial support

We know what it costs a site to wait on a part or a generator that doesn't show up. That's the problem SDT Channel was built to solve — pairing our logistics network with a genuine-parts supply chain and an in-house maintenance team, so equipment owners have one point of contact instead of three separate vendors.

Today we support construction companies, oil & gas operators, manufacturers, government agencies, mining firms, marine operators, engineering firms, and telecom companies across Nigeria — supplying Caterpillar® parts, renting and selling diesel generators, and keeping both running with scheduled maintenance.
